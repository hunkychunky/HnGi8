Who Am I ?

About Me

A backend developer who's stack is in Javascript, NodeJS, Mongo DB, Express JS, Typescript and Rest API. I began programming in the summer of 2016 where i had my first brush with Javascript although it was for frontend web design processes. I proceeded to acquire other skills such as CSS and SASS, then the Bootstrap framework.
The COVID lockdown was an excellent opportunity for me and i decided to make a proper switch from frontend to backend/server side development. My work experience includes two stints at Alpha & Jam - a digital advertising agency where i worked mainly on Graphic design and a content manager. I'm currently participating in Zuri's HNGi8 intenship and this site is one od tasks listed in Stage 2.
